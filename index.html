Technology and Livelihood Education

"The guy is sitting on his desk and is starting to learn about the HTML structure"
"Open the window and press notepad"
"Start by typing <html> to indicate the beginning of the HTML document."
"Now, create the head section by typing <head> immediately after the <html> tag"
"Within the head section, add metadata like the document title and character encoding using <title>...</title> tags."
"Once you've added the necessary metadata, close the head section by typing </head>"
"Now, move on to the body section by typing <body> immediately after the closing <head>"
"Once you've finished adding <body>, close the body section with </body> and the HTML document with </html>"
"Finally, save your file with a meaningful filename and the '.html' extension to indicate it's an HTML document."
